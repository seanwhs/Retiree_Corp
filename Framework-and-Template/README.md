# Open Source Project Artifacts

> Shared wisdom fuels collective creation – artifacts of today shape open pathways. Source the collective mind, let artifacts guide sustainable impact.

This guide outlines the key artifacts for open-source projects in development, training, and consultancy, designed to ensure **clarity, collaboration, and sustainability** while honoring the experience and wisdom of our members.

---

## 1. Technical Artifacts

* **Architecture Diagrams**: Visual representations of system structure and modules to guide understanding and decision-making.
* **API Specifications**: Clear documentation for backend services (OpenAPI/Swagger) to facilitate contribution.
* **Data Models & Schemas**: ER diagrams or JSON schema for datasets and databases, ensuring consistency and transparency.
* **Code Samples & Snippets**: Reusable examples to help members implement features and share best practices.

---

## 2. Project Management Artifacts

* **Risk Register**: Record potential risks and mitigation strategies, drawing on member experience.
* **Dependency Tracker**: Track external libraries, services, and contributor dependencies.
* **Release Notes**: Document features, bug fixes, and version changes for each release.
* **Project Timeline / Milestones**: Visual guides for schedules, deadlines, and key achievements.

---

## 3. Training & Knowledge Artifacts

* **Onboarding Guides**: Step-by-step instructions to welcome new contributors and members.
* **Tutorials & Workshops**: Practical guides for hands-on learning and skill transfer.
* **Mentorship Logs**: Capture mentorship sessions and shared wisdom.
* **FAQ & Troubleshooting Docs**: Answers to common questions, leveraging the collective experience of the community.

---

## 4. Community & Collaboration Artifacts

* **Contribution Guidelines**: Clear expectations, code of conduct, and pull request procedures.
* **Communication Protocols**: Agreed channels, schedules, and etiquette for respectful collaboration.
* **Feedback Logs**: Notes from peer reviews and retrospectives to support continuous improvement.
* **Recognition Records**: Acknowledge member contributions, achievements, and milestones.

---

## 5. Sustainability & Metrics Artifacts

* **Performance Dashboards**: Track KPIs, issue resolution rates, and project progress.
* **Knowledge Retention Docs**: Preserve lessons learned, templates, and best practices for future members.
* **Well-Being Guidelines**: Ensure sustainable workloads, support flexible participation, and maintain member well-being.

---

This artifact framework supports **thoughtful, sustainable, and collaborative open-source projects**, allowing Retiree Corporation members to contribute their knowledge, mentor others, and leave a lasting impact while maintaining balance and well-being.
