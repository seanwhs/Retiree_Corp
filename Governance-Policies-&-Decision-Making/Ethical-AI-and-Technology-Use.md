# Ethical AI & Technology Use

The Retiree Corporation encourages thoughtful integration of AI and technology to enhance contributions while maintaining **human judgment, ethics, and purpose**.

---

## 1. Guidelines for Responsible AI Use

* **Purpose-Driven Integration**: Use AI to augment human work, not replace critical thinking or moral judgment.
* **Ethical Standards**: Ensure AI applications respect privacy, fairness, and transparency.
* **Accountability**: Members remain accountable for decisions influenced by AI tools.
* **Continuous Learning**: Stay informed about AI capabilities, limitations, and evolving best practices.

---

## 2. Balancing Human Judgment and Technology

* **Decision-Making**: Use AI for data processing, predictive analytics, or simulation, but human judgment guides final decisions.
* **Critical Evaluation**: Assess AI outputs critically; avoid blind reliance.
* **Ethical Oversight**: Regularly review AI-assisted processes to ensure alignment with community values and societal impact.

---

## 3. Practical Examples of AI Collaboration

* **Project Planning**: Use AI to model timelines, identify risks, and optimize resource allocation.
* **Knowledge Management**: Employ AI for summarizing documents, highlighting insights, and aiding research.
* **Mentorship Support**: Utilize AI tools to match mentors and mentees based on skills, experience, and availability.
* **Innovation & Exploration**: Leverage AI for brainstorming ideas or simulating scenarios while maintaining human creativity and oversight.

---

By responsibly integrating AI and technology, we ensure our contributions remain ethical, impactful, and aligned with the values of the Retiree Corporation.
